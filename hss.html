Basic Overview

  • Empowering Education

    Our School has been a cornerstone of education in the Kanchan-3, Haraiya community. We have nurtured generations of learners from Early Childhood Development (ECD) to Grade 12. Our mission has always been to empower students with knowledge, skills and confidence.

  • Academic and Holistic Excellence

    We offer a dynamic learning environment with specialized streams in Computer engineering, Management and Education at the secondary level. Our classrooms are supported by modern tools including a library, multimedia resources, and well-equipped science and computer labs.

  • Our Community and Vision

    With 1000+ students, our school is a close-knit community driven by shared dreams. We are committed to innovation, integrity, and empowering every child to thrive and lead.
